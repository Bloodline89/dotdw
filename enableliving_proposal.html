Why three scenarios, not one. SEO forecasts that quote a single number are usually wrong by 2-5×. Conservative is the most cautious projection, based on rank improvements alone. Realistic adds the things a proper programme delivers: better click-through rates, new keywords entering search, and conversion improvements. Strong is the same programme performing well. The real outcome will sit somewhere on this range, and progress is visible in your monthly dashboard.
